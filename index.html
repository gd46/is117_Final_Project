Personalized Tutoring

My name Rob who graduated 2004 from Pope John XXIII Regional High School. I attended Cabrini College, named after Mother Cabrini, and received my Bachelors of Science in Mathematics and a minor in Chemistry. I have been tutoring for 3 years mainly in the subjects of Mathematics. I also work with Physics and Chemistry. I decided to branch off on my own because it seemed that not all students could follow the same regiment. Every student has his/her own strengths and weaknesses. In my sessions we try to find out what the student needs and does not need. Since I have worked with students ranging from 5th grade to 12th grade I have come to understand that each student will require a different approach and I mold the session toward that student. Each student is required to be prepared with homework, textbook, worksheets, and anything else that we may use as reference to progress forward. I like to see past quizzes and exams to help determine where the student may be struggling and if need be we will work from farther back in the year and catch up. My sessions usually are an hour, but upon request the sessions may be longer. Scheduling if mainly on a first come service unless the parent/guardian wishes to plan ahead. I tend to work with multiple students which will force some schedules to change. I do my best to work around the schedule of parents/guardians and their student. Cancellation of a session needs to be minimum 2 hours before scheduled time. Please feel free to contact me from the above information and I look forward to working with you.
